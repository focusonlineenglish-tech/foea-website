Our Course Materials

We teach using two world‑famous Cambridge University Press series. Both series are internationally recognized and suitable for learners who want to improve their English communication skills through a structured learning pathway. You can choose the one that fits your learning style best.

Cambridge Evolve

Evolve is a modern English learning system designed to develop real-world communication skills. It combines speaking, listening, grammar, vocabulary, and critical thinking through interactive and engaging lessons.

Focus: Real‑life communication & fluency

Best for: Students who want to speak naturally and confidently

Levels: A1 to B2 (six levels)

Learn naturally. Speak confidently.

Cambridge Interchange

Interchange is one of the world's most trusted English language programs. It provides a strong foundation in grammar, vocabulary, listening, speaking, reading, and writing through a structured learning path.

Focus: Grammar, vocabulary & structured progress

Best for: Learners who prefer a clear, step‑by‑step method

Levels: Intro to Level 3 (A1–B1)

Step by step. Level by level.

Both series include digital resources, interactive activities, and real‑life tasks. When you join FOEA, we will help you choose the right path for your goals.
